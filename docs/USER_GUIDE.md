# Quantum HR Workflow Assistant — User Guide

## What Is This Tool?

An AI-powered tool for HR professionals in the quantum computing industry. It guides you through a **5-step process** to define, draft, optimize, and plan the execution of a new hire — grounded in a verified database of 3,600+ real quantum job listings.

---

## Getting Started

**Create a workflow** — Click **+** in the tab bar, choose an AI model, and click **Create**. The AI automatically introduces Step 1. You can have up to 10 workflows open in parallel.

**Add your company website** — During Step 1, enter your URL in the bar above the chat and click **Save**. The AI fetches your page description and uses it throughout the conversation.

**Choose a model** — Use the dropdown in the header:
- **Claude Sonnet 4.6** — default, best quality
- **Claude Haiku 4.5** — faster, lighter

**Open the tutorial** — Click the 📖 icon in the top-right corner.

---

## The 5 Steps

### Step 1 · Define the Hiring Need
The AI asks 1–2 questions per turn to pin down: job title, department, seniority, company context, and hiring goal. Once all five are confirmed, the **Next Step** button activates.

### Step 2 · Analyze Job Requirements
The AI produces a requirements table backed by database statistics (e.g. *"68% of listings for this role require a PhD"*). Requirements are flagged as **standard** (>40% of listings) or **rare** (<10%).

### Step 3 · Generate Job Post Draft
The AI first asks 2–3 targeted questions about culture, tone, and benefits — then drafts a structured post with sections: *About the Company · Role Overview · Key Responsibilities · Requirements · What We Offer*. No tables in the draft — only professional prose and bullets.

### Step 4 · Optimization
The AI optimizes for:
- **LinkedIn** — hook-first structure, character limits
- **D&I** — language audit, bias removal
- **ATS / SEO** — keyword density, title normalization
- **Social media** — 280-character teaser formats

All keyword and title alternatives are backed by database frequency data.

### Step 5 · HR Strategy
Every response ends with a **complete, updated roadmap table**:

| Phase | Action | Timeline | Type | Owner | Dependencies |

Type values: 🤖 AI-driven · 👤 Human-driven · 🤝 Hybrid

Click **View Implementation Roadmap** to open the full roadmap popup.

## Navigation

**Step indicator** — Shows all 5 steps. Checkmarks on completed steps, dots on steps with messages. Click any step to review it in read-only mode.

**Next Step button** — Appears only after the AI signals the current step is complete. Clicking it before that signal shows a warning asking you to confirm.

**Quick prompts** — Suggestion chips appear above the input box when the AI is idle. On mobile, tap **Suggestions** to open a panel with the same options.

## Managing Workflows

**Delete** — Click the trash icon on the active tab. A confirmation dialog appears; deletion is permanent.

## Tips

- **Be specific.** Concrete detail produces targeted, data-backed output.
- **Add your company URL** in Step 1 to avoid generic phrasing.
- **Answer Step 3 questions** before asking for the draft — this prevents invented details.
- **Push back freely.** Tell the AI when something feels wrong; it will revise.
- **Ask for database comparisons** at any time — frequency stats, title alternatives, and outlier flags are always available.
